TRAVEL GUIDE

9 DAYS IN WESTWORLD

Day 1

The train will deposit you at Sweetwater, a bustling town full of characters and charm. If you are looking for a glimpse of country life, explore one of Westworld's stunning homesteads, Abernathy Ranch. Home to the lovely Abernathy family, the ranch is also the site of an occasional dramatic showdown.

INSIDE DELOS

Before you step into a seamless guest experience, get a look inside the inner workings of Delos Destinations, Inc.

Day 2

Time to expand your horizons. Kick back with some mezcal at Las Mudas. A close-knit community brimming with history, you'll have chats with the locals that you won't soon forget. The hosts you meet here are representative of the cutting-edge advances in A.I. that have earned Westworld its world-famous reputation.

INSIDE DELOS: EXPLORE THE MESA

Looking for a new career? Delos is recruiting. Check out our state-of-the-art facilities where staffers live during their Westworld work rotations.

Day 3

If you're looking for some adventure, head to Python Pass: the perfect place for hiking, animal watching, and mining for gold. Be sure to dress accordingly; the Pass is one of the most strenuous climbing experiences in the park. The natural beauty only gets better once the sun goes down; get a glimpse of the breathtaking constellations that aren't dulled by city lights. No need to fear the darkness-our hosts are always monitored and contained.

INSIDE DELOS: NAVIGATING NARRATIVES

Crafting 100 interconnected storylines is no small feat. Find out more about how the endless potential narratives play out here.

Day 4

Our park is ever-evolving, so stop by our famed Agave Plantation, while you still can. A treasured oasis for years, this tequila distillery gives guests a glimpse at the refined culture and decadent charm of agrarian life. The grounds are a good reminder that beneath the surface, plenty of surprising discoveries await.

INSIDE DELOS: CIRCLES OF LIFE

The park is a richly-woven tapestry of thousands of closely-stitched interconnected loops. See how we hide the seams and keep you immersed in our world.

Day 5

Time to lean into one of the park's most legendary perks: guilt-free sin. Decadance knows no bounds in Pariah, Westworld's capital of sensuality. Pick a side, as war lurks on the fringes of town, but choose carefully: there is no law. While you wait for dates to open up, get in the mood with musical favorites from the Mariposa.

Day 6

In the midst of all the action, guests often overlook the most nuanced details of the park. Take a minute to discover something you hadn't noticed before. As you explore the park, you may notice we are breaking ground on our most daring narrative yet. This storyline is guaranteed to bring out the good, bad and ugly... depending on how you play it.

Day 7

After a week in the park, you may be ready to explore sections designed for more experienced park visitors. One such area is Ghost Nation Territory; it's home to the fierce tribe, so enter at your own risk. As you dive in, be prepared to have your limits tested. Westworld storylines are complex and oftentimes difficult decisions stand in the way of your chosen objective. Some people may be left behind in the process; how far are you willing to go?

INSIDE DELOS: SAFETY GUARANTEED

How are you protected from explosions, weapons and trigger-happy black hats? See how Quality Assurance works to keep you safe.

Day 8

Ready for the stuff of legend? Find your way to the Unclaimed Territories. Word is that a quaint town full of friendly people existed in these parts, but there is no evidence to support those claims. Few have made it this far and even fewer have returned.

INSIDE WESTWORLD: MUSIC TO YOUR EARS

Get a look at how the Mariposa's signature sound is composed.

Day 9

Should you feel inclined to wear the black hat, head to Sweetwater Hills. Known to be the hideout of murderous criminal Hector Escaton, the Hills offer refuge from the law, but that won't mean you're safe. Remember that anyone you meet could be your next accomplice; all our hosts exist to serve you.

INSIDE DELOS: HOST CREATION

Delos strives to redefine the limits of design and technology. Get a glimpse at how feats of engineering manufacture something better than reality.
